# Sources And Verification Notes

Prepared: 2026-06-30

## Source Standard

This pack prioritises official Irish public-agency sources and direct programme pages. Grant conditions change, and Local Enterprise Office interpretation can vary by county. Recheck every eligibility point before filing a company form, signing founder documents, incurring grant-funded costs, or submitting a grant application.

## Company Formation

- CRO, [Required Steps](https://cro.ie/registration/company/required-steps/)
  - Used for CRO incorporation route, including Form A1 and constitution through CORE.
- CRO, [Company Types](https://cro.ie/registration/company/company-types/)
  - Used for LTD features: 1 to 149 members, one-document constitution, one director possible with separate secretary, no AGM requirement.
- CRO, [Registering a Company](https://cro.ie/registration/company/)
  - Used for incorporation documents and Section 137 bond reference if no EEA-resident director.
- CRO, [European Economic Area Resident Director](https://cro.ie/information-leaflets/european-economic-area-resident-director-leaflet-17/)
  - Used for the requirement to have at least one EEA-resident director unless an exception/bond route applies.
- CRO, [Filing an Annual Return](https://cro.ie/annual-return/filing-an-annual-return/)
  - Used for first annual return timing.

## Beneficial Ownership

- RBO, [What is a Beneficial Owner?](https://rbo.gov.ie/faqs/what-is-a-beneficial-owner-other-information/)
  - Used for beneficial ownership thresholds: 25% plus one share, more than 25% voting rights, more than 25% ownership interest, or control by other means.
- CRO, [Registering Beneficial Ownership](https://cro.ie/registration/beneficial-ownership/)
  - Used for the five-month filing deadline for newly incorporated entities.
- RBO, [PPSN and VIF/IPN guidance](https://rbo.gov.ie/faqs/ppsn-why-its-required-and-what-to-do-if-you-dont-have-one/)
  - Used for Alex/non-Irish PPSN identity verification notes.
- CRO, [Beneficial Owners With No PPSN](https://cro.ie/registration/beneficial-ownership/rbo-no-ppsn/)
  - Used for the IPN/VIF process.
