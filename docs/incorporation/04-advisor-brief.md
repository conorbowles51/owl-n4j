# Advisor Brief

Prepared: 2026-06-30

## Reader And Use

This brief is for an Irish startup solicitor, Irish accountant, Enterprise Ireland/LEO/BIC advisor, and Alex's US tax advisor. It gives the facts and questions needed to advise on incorporation, founder ownership, IP assignment, and funding eligibility.

## Short Version

Three founders are building an AI-powered investigation platform.

- Conor lives in Ireland and is a developer/founder.
- Neil lives in Ireland and is a developer/founder.
- Alex lives in the United States and is a domain/commercial founder.
- Alex has an existing US business called Owl Consultancy.
- The founders want to incorporate in Ireland.
- The intended ownership is one third Conor, one third Neil, one third Alex.
- The platform should be owned by the Irish product company.
- Owl Consultancy may be involved commercially, but the founders do not want it to accidentally own or control the platform.
- The company wants to apply for Irish grants/state investment to support continued development, modest founder pay, later marketing, and potentially hiring.

## Requested Outcome

Advise on the cleanest structure to:

1. Incorporate an Irish LTD.
2. Give Conor, Neil, and Alex equal founder economics.
3. Keep company management, control, IP, and key functions in Ireland for Enterprise Ireland/LEO eligibility.
4. Include Alex without creating unnecessary US/Ireland tax or grant issues.
5. Separate Alex personally from Owl Consultancy's commercial role.
6. Put founder IP, brand, code, documentation, deployment assets, and product know-how into the Irish company.
7. Preserve future ability to apply for New Frontiers, PSSF, LEO grants, Innovation Voucher, IP Start, R&D tax credit, and later HPSU/private investment.

## Proposed Default Structure

- Irish LTD as parent and product company.
- Conor, Neil, and Alex as equal ordinary shareholders.
- Alex holds shares personally unless US tax advice recommends Owl Consultancy or another vehicle.
- Conor and/or Neil act as Irish/EEA-resident director(s).
- Alex may be shareholder only, board observer, or director depending on advice.
- Product IP assigned to Irish LTD.
- Owl Consultancy documented separately if it is a customer, referrer, reseller, or service provider.
- Shareholders agreement includes reverse vesting/leaver rules, transfer restrictions, reserved matters, deadlock, drag/tag, IP, confidentiality, and conflict rules.
