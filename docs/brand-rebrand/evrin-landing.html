Identity direction

Operational workflow

From evidence intake to defensible output.

01

Ingest and extract

Bring in files, phone reports, financial records, images, and notes. Evrin turns raw material into searchable evidence with structured metadata.

02

Resolve and connect

Entities, dates, locations, transactions, and communications resolve into linked case views for analysts, reviewers, and field teams.

03

Report with sources

Every narrative, chart, and agent-created artifact keeps its source trail visible so conclusions can be checked before they leave the room.

Enterprise posture

Built for sensitive case teams, not casual AI search.

For private investigation firms, legal teams, forensic accountants, and complex-review groups, Evrin presents the work as controlled intelligence: governed access, source trails, deadlines, and analyst-ready case structure.

Source trails Findings carry citations back to the case material that supports them.
Case governance Workspaces, collaborators, deadlines, and review states stay organized by matter.
Audit-friendly output Reports and agent artifacts are built for review before escalation.
Specialist views Graph, timeline, map, table, financial, and device workflows sit in one operating layer.
