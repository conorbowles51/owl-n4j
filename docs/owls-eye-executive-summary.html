The Owl's Eye Platform

Executive Summary for Seed Investment

Owl Consultancy Group is a Washington, DC-based private investigations firm founded by investigators specializing in complex federal criminal defense, white collar crime, financial fraud, and corporate due diligence. We work alongside attorneys and defense teams on high-stakes matters, and to meet the analytical demands of our own caseload, we built The Owl's Eye—proprietary AI investigation software that automatically ingests thousands of documents, extracts entities and relationships, maps networks, reconstructs timelines, and surfaces hidden connections. It is already deployed and generating results on live cases. We are seeking $1.5M in seed investment to commercialize and spin off The Owl's Eye: a platform built by practitioners, proven in the field, and addressing a massive, underserved market.

The Problem

Investigators, attorneys, compliance teams, and fraud analysts face the same crisis: the evidence they need to win exists—it's buried inside thousands of documents, financial records, and communications they simply don't have the time or resources to manually read and connect. A federal case can generate 500,000 pages of discovery. Every hour spent on manual document review is an hour not spent on strategy—and at investigator rates of $300–$500/hour and legal fees exceeding $1,000/hour, the cost is devastating. Worse, critical connections get missed. Cases are lost. Fraud goes undetected.

The Solution

The Owl's Eye replaces manual document review with automated intelligence. Upload thousands of documents, financial records, audio files, or communications—the platform does the rest. Automated entity & relationship extraction identifies every person, company, account, and location and maps how they connect. Interactive network visualization reveals hidden connections no manual review would catch. Timeline reconstruction organizes events automatically. AI investigation assistant allows natural language questions directly against the evidence. One-click reports generate comprehensive, citation-backed investigative reports instantly. On-premise deployment keeps all data within the client's environment, protecting confidentiality.

Target Market & Customer Segments

  • Litigation teams in major law firms
